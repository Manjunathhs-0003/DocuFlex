Learn More About DocuFlex

Introduction

DocuFlex is your one-stop solution for managing your fleet's documents and records. Our application is designed to streamline your operations, ensuring your fleet stays on the road with minimal downtime.

Detailed Features

Vehicle Management

Add, edit, and track details of all vehicles in your fleet with ease, ensuring you always have up-to-date information.

Document Management

Securely store and manage all vehicle-related documents, including registration, insurance, permits, and inspection records.

Insurance Tracking

Monitor and manage insurance policies, ensuring timely renewals and compliance with regulations.

Automatic Reminders

Set up automatic reminders for document renewals, inspections, and other critical dates to keep your fleet compliant.

Secure Access

Ensure data privacy and secure access to information with state-of-the-art security features.
